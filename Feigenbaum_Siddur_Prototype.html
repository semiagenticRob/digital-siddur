שְׁמֹנֶה עֶשְׂרֵה
Shemoneh Esrei · Shacharis
⚑ Prototype. Hebrew is standard public-domain liturgy. English is placeholder copy to show the experience — the published Feigenbaum commentary awaits digitization & author/rav proofing. Highlights & notes are in-memory here; the real app stores them on your device.
Opening
אֲדֹנָי שְׂפָתַי תִּפְתָּח, וּפִי יַגִּיד תְּהִלָּתֶךָ.
Placeholder explanationBefore we say a word, we ask Hashem to open our lips — admitting that even the ability to talk to Him is a gift He gives us.
1 · Avos · אבות
בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ אֱלֹהֵינוּ וֵאלֹהֵי אֲבוֹתֵינוּ, אֱלֹהֵי אַבְרָהָם, אֱלֹהֵי יִצְחָק, וֵאלֹהֵי יַעֲקֹב,
הָאֵל הַגָּדוֹל הַגִּבּוֹר וְהַנּוֹרָא, אֵל עֶלְיוֹן, גּוֹמֵל חֲסָדִים טוֹבִים, וְקוֹנֵה הַכֹּל,
וְזוֹכֵר חַסְדֵי אָבוֹת, וּמֵבִיא גוֹאֵל לִבְנֵי בְנֵיהֶם לְמַעַן שְׁמוֹ בְּאַהֲבָה.
מֶלֶךְ עוֹזֵר וּמוֹשִׁיעַ וּמָגֵן. בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ, מָגֵן אַבְרָהָם.
Placeholder explanationWe don't walk up to Hashem as strangers. We come as the children of Avraham, Yitzchak and Yaakov — reminding ourselves we're part of something that started long before us.
Placeholder insight. Why start with the Avos and not just jump to our requests? Because relationships come before requests — we ground the conversation in who we are before we ask for anything.
2 · Gevuros · גבורות
אַתָּה גִּבּוֹר לְעוֹלָם אֲדֹנָי, מְחַיֵּה מֵתִים אַתָּה, רַב לְהוֹשִׁיעַ.
בַּחֹרֶף (in winter) add:
מַשִּׁיב הָרוּחַ וּמוֹרִיד הַגֶּשֶׁם.
מְכַלְכֵּל חַיִּים בְּחֶסֶד, מְחַיֵּה מֵתִים בְּרַחֲמִים רַבִּים, סוֹמֵךְ נוֹפְלִים וְרוֹפֵא חוֹלִים וּמַתִּיר אֲסוּרִים,
וּמְקַיֵּם אֱמוּנָתוֹ לִישֵׁנֵי עָפָר. מִי כָמוֹךָ בַּעַל גְּבוּרוֹת וּמִי דּוֹמֶה לָּךְ,
מֶלֶךְ מֵמִית וּמְחַיֶּה וּמַצְמִיחַ יְשׁוּעָה. בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ, מְחַיֵּה הַמֵּתִים.
Placeholder explanationThis bracha is about Hashem's power — but notice where it goes: holding up the falling, healing the sick, freeing the trapped. Real strength, here, looks like care.

Notice the line in clay above the rain insert — that's a conditional rubric. In v1 it's shown as a label; in the phase-2 companion the app will know the date and show the right text automatically.
3 · Kedushas Hashem · קדושת השם
אַתָּה קָדוֹשׁ וְשִׁמְךָ קָדוֹשׁ, וּקְדוֹשִׁים בְּכָל יוֹם יְהַלְלוּךָ סֶּלָה.
בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ, הָאֵל הַקָּדוֹשׁ.
Placeholder explanation"Holy" means set apart — beyond anything we can fully picture. We pause here to remember Who we're actually talking to.
4 · Binah · בינה
אַתָּה חוֹנֵן לְאָדָם דַּעַת, וּמְלַמֵּד לֶאֱנוֹשׁ בִּינָה.
חָנֵּנוּ מֵאִתְּךָ דֵּעָה בִּינָה וְהַשְׂכֵּל. בָּרוּךְ אַתָּה יְיָ, חוֹנֵן הַדָּעַת.
Placeholder explanationThe very first thing we ask for is the ability to think clearly. Everything else we'll request depends on it — so we start here.
Placeholder insight. Asking for understanding first is a quiet statement: I can't fix my life on autopilot. Clear thinking is the tool every other request runs on.
